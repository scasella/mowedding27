The weekend

A Friday well spent, then a whole weekend.

We’re marrying on a Friday evening — so come a little early, stay a little late, and make a weekend of it in the New Jersey countryside. Times below are a working draft; we’ll lock them in as the day gets closer.

ThursdayEvening

Come a day early

Around town · Hunterdon County

Travelling from afar? Thursday is the easy-arrival day. Settle into your hotel, find dinner at a country tavern, and beat the Friday rush. We’ll point you to our favorites on the Travel page.

Friday4:00 pm

Welcome drinks

The garden terrace · arrive from 3:30

A glass of something cold to start the evening. Find a familiar face, meet the people from the other half of the story, and let the day open slowly.

Friday5:00 pm

The ceremony

Under the oaks · seats provided

The part we flew 36 hours and waited five years for. Please be seated by a quarter to five — it’s short, and we mean every word of it.

Friday6:00 pm

Cocktails & the golden hour

Lawn & veranda

Drinks, small bites, and the best light of the day. Photographs will be happening; so will hugs.

Friday7:00 pm

Dinner & dancing

The barn · seated dinner, then the floor

A long, generous dinner, a few words that may make you cry, and then a dance floor we fully intend to wear out. Dress code: garden formal — suits and floor-or-cocktail-length dresses; leave the stilettos, the lawn has opinions.

Friday11:00 pm

The send-off

Front drive

A last song, a small spectacle, and a shuttle back to the hotels for anyone who’d like one.

Saturday10:00 am

Farewell breakfast

Hotel · drop in anytime ’til noon

Coffee, pastries, and a soft landing before the drive home. No agenda — just one more cup together before everyone scatters.

One small thing

Stay for all of it. The best parts of this wedding are the ones that aren’t on the schedule.
