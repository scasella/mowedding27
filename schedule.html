The weekend

A Friday evening, and the weekend around it.

We’re marrying on a Friday evening, so come early and stay through the weekend in the New Jersey countryside. The times below are a working draft; we’ll confirm them closer to the day.

ThursdayEvening

Come a day early

Around town · Hunterdon County

Coming from far away? Thursday is the gentlest day to arrive. Settle into your hotel and find dinner at a country tavern before everyone else gets in. A few of our favorites are on the Travel page.

Friday4:00 pm

Welcome drinks

The garden terrace · arrive from 3:30

A glass of something cold to begin the evening. Find a familiar face, meet the half of the room you haven’t met yet, and let the evening open slowly.

Friday5:00 pm

The ceremony

Under the oaks · seats provided

The reason we’re all here. Please be seated by a quarter to five; it will be short, and entirely meant.

Friday6:00 pm

Cocktails & the golden hour

Lawn & veranda

Drinks, small bites, and the best light of the day.

Friday7:00 pm

Dinner & dancing

The barn · seated dinner, then the floor

A long, generous dinner, a few toasts, and a dance floor we fully intend to wear out. Dress code: garden formal — suits and floor- or cocktail-length dresses. Much of the evening is on grass, so flats or block heels will serve you better than stilettos.

Friday11:00 pm

The send-off

Front drive

A last song, a proper send-off, and a shuttle back to the hotels for anyone who’d like one.

Saturday10:00 am

Farewell breakfast

Hotel · drop in anytime ’til noon

Coffee, pastries, and an unhurried morning before the drive home. No agenda — just one more cup together before everyone heads off.

One small thing

Stay for all of it. The best moments tend not to be the ones on the schedule.
