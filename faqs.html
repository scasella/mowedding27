Good questions

Everything you’re about to ask.

We’re the kind of hosts who like to answer the question before you’ve had to send the text. Here’s the short version; if we’ve missed something, just reach out — we’d love to hear from you.

First, the essentials

Wait — it’s on a Friday?

It is — Friday, June 18, 2027, in the evening. We know a weekday takes a little planning, so we’re telling you early and asking you to make a proper weekend of it. Come Thursday or Friday morning, stay through Saturday, and let New Jersey show off a little.

Meghan

Take the Friday off. You’ll thank yourself.

Owen

And the Monday, honestly. No one regrets the Monday.

Where exactly is Whitehouse Station?

It’s a small community in Hunterdon County, in the rolling western part of New Jersey — about an hour’s drive west of Newark Airport. Beautiful, rural, and genuinely worth the trip. Full directions, airports, and drive times live on the Travel page.

What time should I arrive?

Welcome drinks open at 4:00 pm and the ceremony begins at 5:00 — please be seated by 4:45. The full run of the evening is on the Schedule.

The day itself

What’s the dress code?

Garden formal. Suits and floor- or cocktail-length dresses; summer colors are very welcome. One practical note: parts of the evening are on grass, so kind shoes will serve you better than stilettos.

Meghan

Block heels or flats. The lawn is undefeated.

Owen

I’m wearing the comfortable shoes. Join me.

Will it be indoors or out?

Both. The ceremony and cocktails are outdoors under the oaks (weather permitting), and dinner and dancing move into a restored barn. June evenings can cool off after sunset, so a light layer is a smart idea.

Is there parking? Can I leave my car overnight?

Yes — there’s ample free parking on site, and you’re welcome to leave your car overnight and collect it the next day. We’ll also run a shuttle to the nearby hotels at the end of the night, so please don’t drive if you’ve been celebrating.

Plus-ones, kids & the guest list

Can I bring a plus-one?

Your invitation will name everyone we’ve saved a seat for — so if a guest is included, it’ll say so right on your RSVP. If you’re unsure, just ask us; we’re easy to reach and happy to help.

Are kids invited?

We adore the little ones in our lives — and this one’s a grown-ups’ evening.

Meghan

Adults only, with love.

Owen

Consider it a date night on us. Get the good babysitter.

How do I RSVP, and by when?

Our RSVP form opens in early 2027 — head to the RSVP page and we’ll walk you through it (attending, guests, meal choice, a song request, and a note if you like). For now, the only thing to do is hold the date: Friday, June 18.

Getting here & staying over

Do I need to rent a car?

We’d strongly recommend it. The venue and hotels are spread across the countryside and rideshares are thin on the ground out here. Pick up a car at the airport — you’ll want the freedom. See Travel for the details.

Where should I stay?

We’re holding room blocks at a couple of nearby hotels, with a few charming inns a little farther out if you’d like to make a weekend of it. All of them, with booking links, are on the Travel page.

Is there a gift registry?

There is — though truthfully, your being there is the gift. If you’d like to do more, the Registry has a small, well-loved list, plus a honeymoon fund to send us back to the Aegean (it’s a long story; it’s a good one).

Still wondering about something?

Reach out anytime at meghanandowen@example.com — we promise a real, fast answer.
