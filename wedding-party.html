The wedding party

The people who got us here.

Some of them you’ll recognize from the story — the friend who heard “I’m going to marry him” one week in, the roommate who set the whole butterfly effect in motion, the mom who spent a whole day touring a groom around a Greek island. The rest you’ll be glad to meet.

On Meghan’s side

Sophia

Maid of Honor

The first person to hear the words “I’m going to marry him” — one week in, and she didn’t laugh. She was on the island for the proposal, in on every part of it. If there’s a plan, Sophia is three steps into it already.

Edie

Bridesmaid · where it all began

The roommate Meghan almost didn’t live with — the one whose chain of events introduced these two in the first place. Five years later she was right there on the clifftop. The whole butterfly effect, standing in a bridesmaid dress.

Priya

Bridesmaid · the sister

Meghan’s sister and built-in best friend, keeper of the family stories and the only one allowed to tease the bride on the day. Expect a toast.

Hannah

Bridesmaid

The friend who turns an ordinary Tuesday into a story worth retelling. Loyal to a fault and funny on purpose.

On Owen’s side

Jack

Best Man

Owen’s brother, co-pilot, and the man who quietly helped plan a 36-hour secret flight without cracking once. If you need anything on the day, find Jack.

Marcus

Groomsman

Friends since the dorm days. The calm one in any crisis and the first one on the dance floor — somehow both at once.

Theo

Groomsman

The one with the camera, the playlist, and the road-trip energy. He’s been documenting this friendship for a decade; the wedding is just the next chapter.

Daniel

Groomsman

Steady, generous, and endlessly game. The kind of friend who shows up early and stays late, every single time.

A special thank-you

The co-conspirators

No proposal pulls itself off. This one took an entire island — and two people in particular. Sophia’s mom spent an entire day touring Owen quietly around Sifnos so the surprise would land at exactly the right moment, in exactly the right light. And Meghan’s family, who kept a straight face through a whole vacation while a groom flew 36 hours to crash it.

If you see them this weekend, buy them a drink. We owe them the best one of our lives.
