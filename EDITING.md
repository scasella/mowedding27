# Editing your wedding website

Hi Meghan & Owen — here's how to update **mowedding27.com** yourselves. No coding,
nothing to install. You edit in a simple form and your changes go live on their own.

**First time only:** Stephen will send you an email invitation. Click the link in it
and **choose a password** (or pick "Sign in with Google / Microsoft"). That's your
login from then on — you do **not** need a GitHub account or anything technical.

## How to make a change

1. Go to **https://mowedding27.com/admin** (bookmark it).
2. **Log in with your email and password** (the ones from your invitation).
3. In the left menu, pick the part of the site you want to change:
   - **Site & home** — your date, venue, the homepage welcome and your story
   - **Engagement photos** — the photos in the homepage ribbon and gallery
   - **Schedule** — the timeline of the weekend
   - **FAQs** — questions & answers
   - **Travel & stays** — airports, hotels, things to do
   - **Registry** — the honeymoon fund and store links
   - **Wedding party** — names, roles, and headshots
4. Edit the text, or swap a photo, in the form.
5. Click **Save**, then **Publish** (it may say "Publish now").
6. **Wait about 1–2 minutes**, then refresh **https://mowedding27.com** in a normal
   browser tab. Your change appears once the site finishes updating. It's normal for
   it not to be instant.

## A few tips

- **Edit on a laptop** for anything involving photos — it's much easier than a phone.
- **One of you at a time**, to avoid edit clashes.
- Photos: any normal photo works; the site handles sizing. When you add a photo,
  also fill in the short **"Alt text"** (a sentence describing the photo) — it helps
  screen readers and shows if an image ever fails to load.
- **If something looks wrong, don't keep clicking Publish** — text Stephen. The previous
  version is always saved and can be put back in one click. You can't break anything permanently.

## What you can't change here

The page layout, fonts, colors, and the little decorative flourishes are fixed (that's
the design). If you want one of those changed, just ask Stephen. Everything in the list
above — the words, dates, photos, schedule, FAQs, travel, registry, and party — is yours
to edit anytime.
